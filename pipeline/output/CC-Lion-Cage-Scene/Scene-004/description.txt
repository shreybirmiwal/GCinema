A man with dark hair and a small mustache, dressed in a light collared shirt, dark suspenders, and baggy trousers, is trapped inside a large enclosure made of vertical metal bars. To his right, a large adult male lion lies completely motionless on the floor, fast asleep. A sign reading DANGEROUS is visible just outside the enclosure. The man stands on the left side holding a thin cane, his face contorted in utter panic. He frantically pulls at the metal bars and reaches upward, desperately trying to force an escape route while constantly glancing back at the sleeping animal. After failing to open the barrier, he moves to another section of the cage. Wide-eyed and trembling with fear, he presses his back and hands completely flat against the vertical bars. He then slowly and cautiously inches sideways along the perimeter, sliding from the right toward the left, attempting to move as quietly as possible to avoid waking the lion.
