A slender man stands inside a confined structure enclosed by thick vertical metal bars. He wears a white short-sleeved shirt, a dark vest, baggy trousers, suspenders, and a small bow tie. He has dark curly hair and a prominent small mustache. The man presses his hands flat against a closed wooden door on his left. He abruptly turns his head over his right shoulder to look through the bars. His eyes widen and his mouth opens in an expression of sudden fear. He freezes in this panicked stance, staring intensely at an unseen threat. After a brief hesitation, he quickly looks back at the door, then snaps his head over his shoulder again for a rapid double-take. Yielding to his panic, he violently pivots his entire body to the left and throws himself against the wooden door in a desperate attempt to escape.
